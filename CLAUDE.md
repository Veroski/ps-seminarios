Preset E — "Aurum Clinic" (Clinical Prestige)

Identity:
A private medical institute where clinical precision meets quiet luxury. Think Zurich longevity clinic + luxury dermatology institute. Sterile, calm, highly controlled — but undeniably premium. Less nightlife gold, more medical-grade elegance.

This aesthetic communicates trust, expertise, precision, and exclusivity. It feels like a place where procedures cost five figures and everything is measured, calibrated, and deliberate.

Palette

Primary: Surgical White #F7F7F5

Secondary: Titanium #B8B8B2

Accent: Aurum Gold #C6A75C

Depth / Dark: Graphite #1F1F1F

Soft Background: Porcelain #ECEBE7

Color Logic

Backgrounds stay clean, medical, almost laboratory white

Gold is used sparingly (CTA, highlights, separators)

Typography and UI rely on graphite / titanium instead of pure black

The feeling should be clinical calm, not dramatic darkness.

Typography

Headings: "Neue Haas Grotesk Display" or "Inter" (tight tracking)

Drama / Hero: "Canela" or "Playfair Display" Italic

Data / Metrics: "IBM Plex Mono"

Typography Personality

Headlines feel editorial medical journal

Serif italics introduce luxury and softness

Monospace provides clinical instrumentation vibe
